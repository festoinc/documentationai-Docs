## Overview

The **BookingPal Supplier API** enables PMS (Property Management System) providers and Property Managers (PMs) to programmatically manage vacation rental properties, rates, availability, reservations, images, messaging, and more.

<Callout kind="info">
All responses are returned as JSON. The maximum payload per request is **200 KB**. Successful `DELETE` requests return a `200` status with an empty response body.
</Callout>

## Before You Begin

1. Your organization must be **registered and activated** with BookingPal.
2. You must have completed a **kick-off meeting**.
3. The **opening questionnaire** must be filled out.
4. You must have your **login and password** credentials.
